Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 22, 2025

About Us
IUV GameDev ("we", "us", or "our") is an independent game developer creating baby and preschool children’s apps published on the Google Play Store.

Scope
This Privacy Policy applies to all games published under the IUV GameDev account on the Google Play Store.

Information We Collect
We do not collect any personal information from users. We do not use analytics or any online databases like Firebase or Firestore. Any game progress, settings such as sound and volume, or preferences are stored locally on your device only.

Advertising
We use Google AdMob to display ads within our apps. We do not use personalized ads and do not collect or share data for personalized advertising. Ads are shown equally to all users.

Cross-Promotion
We may promote our other games within our apps via in-app cross-promotion. All promotions link only to Google Play Store pages of our games. No external links or third-party game links are used.

Children’s Privacy
Our apps are designed specifically for children under the age of 13. We do not collect any personal information from children or adults. We do not allow in-app purchases or any features that could lead to accidental or intentional spending by children.

Cookies and Tracking
We do not use cookies, location data, login/account systems, social media integrations, or any tracking tools.

Opting Out
Since we do not collect personal data or serve personalized ads, no opt-out is necessary. For more information on Google Ads, please visit:
